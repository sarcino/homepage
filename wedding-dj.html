Find the DJ for your wedding

Design an app which will help users to book wedding DJs.

This project was created for the UX Design Google Certification Course on Coursera.

The Challenge

There are too many DJs on the internet on different websites and there is no easy way to contact them.

The Goal

Create a straightforward workflow to send a request to the DJ without long browsing and searching for contact.

Speed up the process of wedding DJs booking

User Pain Points

Too many sources

There is no one place where you can find various DJs and book their services.

Missing Information

It is difficult to find reviews.

Additional services

It is difficult to request additional services from the DJ like facilitation of the event.

Samples not available

Users have to visit official pages of DJs to check samples of their work.

Digital Wireframes & Low Fidelity Prototype

Every DJ will have their own profile where the user can find a short description, samples, review and price range for additional services.

Reviews will show how satisfied were other brides and grooms with the DJ. It should help users to choose the right DJ for their weddings.

The application will also include the budget filter. Users can filter DJs based on their budget preferences.

The main workflow should be simple. A user fills in the search form, selects a DJ, request a DJ and wait for the response from the selected artist.

Usability Study

Moderated usability study with 5 participants from the Czech Republic. Users tested the first version of the low fidelity prototype.

Buttons, filtering, and tags

More details link is too small.

Filtering should be displayed clearer way.

Genre tags look like buttons and users do not understand them

I removed “More details” link under each DJ profile and add a large arrow instead. It is easier to tap on and increase accessibility of the application.

Workflow

Workflow is not clear to users. They don’t understand what is the next step after sending message to the selected DJ.

I changed copy of the button to “Send a request” instead of “Book the DJ” and displayed the workflow navigation.
